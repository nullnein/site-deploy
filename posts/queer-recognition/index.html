DW - Fighting for recognition of queer prosecution during National Socialism in Germany

By mathis
Posted Jan 27 1 min read

News-portrayal of Lutz van Dijk who fought for years that the German Bundestag recognize the prosecution of queer people during National Socialism. On January 27th 2023, the German parliament held it’s annual rememberance ceremony with a special focus on the prosecution of sexual minorities.

TV News piece running throughout the day, here as a piece during lives

Social piece delivered to premium partners and language departments

On the International Holocaust Rememberance Day, the German Bundestag heard from both Jewish and other victims of the National Socialists.
